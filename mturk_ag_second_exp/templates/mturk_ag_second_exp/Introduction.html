Instructions

You are invited to participate in an experiment, which is part of a research conducted in the Industrial Engineering and Management Faculty of the Technion, Haifa.

You have been randomly and anonymously paired with another participant (that can either be human or a robot). You were randomly selected to be the Decision Maker, while your partner is the Expert.

The experiment consists of {{ Constants.num_rounds }} trials. All trials will be played with the same partner. You will not switch roles during the whole experiment.

In each trial, you will be asked to choose one of 2 lotteries: the ‘Known’ and the ‘Unknown’. Both provide a gain or a loss.

You will get all the information about the ‘Known’ lottery.

You will get no prior information about the probability to win the ‘Unknown’ lottery. The expert will have some information and will give you an estimate of this probability.

In each trial, the expected payoffs of the ‘known’ lottery will be 0 (that is, on average, the payoff from 'Known' is 0).

Compensation:

Your payoff in each trial, is the outcome of your chosen lottery. The expert will gain 1 point if you choose 'Unknown', and 0 otherwise.

You will get {{ participation_fee }} for your participation.

Your goal is to maximize your earnings.

You will get {{ initial_points_dc }} points at the beginning of the experiment.

At the end of the experiment, the accumulated points will set your probability to get a bonus of ${{ Constants.bonus }} (the probability of a bonus increases with the accumulated number of points).

Deadline:

You will have {{ Constants.seconds_wait_first_rounds }} seconds to respond in the first {{ Constants.num_rounds_long_wait }} trials, and {{ Constants.seconds_wait }} seconds in the following trials. If you will not meet these deadlines, one of the options will be chosen randomly.

Risks and benefits:

There is no risk. Your benefit is contributing to a research project.

Voluntary participation and right to withdraw:

Participation in this study is voluntary, and you can stop at any time without any penalty. To stop, click on the “Return HIT” button, or close your browser window.

Contact information:

If you have any concerns or questions about this research, you may contact: Reut Apel at reutapel@campus.technion.ac.il.
